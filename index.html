What is Mediac?

Mediac is an app that allows you to search for any sickness and get fast, reliable medication recommendations.

What makes us special?

  • Voice-activated search anywhere
  • Immediate medicine suggestions and prices
  • AI-powered personalized guidance
  • Accessories that connect instantly and reliably

How it works

Simply open the app, type or speak your symptoms, and get medication info in seconds. Track your medication, set reminders, and stay healthy with ease.
